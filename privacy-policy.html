Privacy Policy

At compoundcalc.co.za, the privacy of our visitors is of extreme importance to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information received and collected by compoundcalc.co.za and how it is used.


Log Files and Analytics

Like many websites, we use log files and Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors engage with our tool. Information collected includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date/time stamp, referring/exit pages, and number of clicks. This information is used to analyse trends, administer the site, and gather demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any personally identifiable information.


Cookies and Web Beacons

compoundcalc.co.za uses cookies to store information about visitors' preferences and to record user-specific information about which pages the user accesses or visits. You can choose to disable cookies through your individual browser settings.


Google AdSense and the DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads on compoundcalc.co.za. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our visitors based upon their visit to compoundcalc.co.za and other sites on the internet. Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google ad and content network privacy policy at: http://www.google.com/privacy_ads.html.


What Data We Do Not Collect

The CompoundCalc calculator does not collect, store, or transmit any of the numbers you enter. All calculation inputs (principal, rate, years, monthly contribution) exist only in your browser session and are never sent to our servers.


Third-Party Services

This site uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics 4 — anonymous usage analytics
  • Google AdSense — display advertising (once live)
  • Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — email delivery, if you request a PDF projection
  • EasyEquities / Trading 212 — affiliate referral links

Your Rights (POPIA)

Under the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), South African residents have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@compoundcalc.co.za.
