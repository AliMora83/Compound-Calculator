Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Introduction

At CompoundCalc.co.za, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly contact us. The data you input into the calculator (e.g., principal, contributions, interest rates) is processed locally in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

3. Cookies and Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors interact with our site. This helps us improve the user experience. GA4 collects anonymous usage data, such as page views and button clicks. We also use cookies to store your currency preference and whether you have accepted our cookie banner.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services such as:

  • Google AdSense: To serve relevant advertisements (once active).
  • Affiliate Platforms: Such as EasyEquities or Trading 212, which may track clicks via cookies to attribute referrals.

5. Your Rights

Under POPIA, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of any personal data we may hold about you. Since we do not store personal data from your calculations, this primarily applies to email communications. To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@compoundcalc.co.za.

6. Cookie Consent

By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies for analytics and performance. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.
