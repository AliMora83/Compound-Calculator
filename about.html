About CompoundCalc

CompoundCalc is a free, interactive financial tool designed to help you visualize the power of compound interest. Our mission is to make complex financial projections accessible to everyone, helping you plan your path to financial freedom with clarity and confidence.

We believe that time is the most powerful tool in an investor's arsenal. By understanding how small, regular contributions can grow over decades, you can make more informed decisions about your savings and investments today. Whether you're saving for retirement, a new home, or your children's education, CompoundCalc provides the data you need to stay on track.

The tool was built primarily for South African investors, accounting for local considerations like inflation adjustments and common contribution frequencies, but it functions perfectly for users globally. CompoundCalc is entirely free to use and requires no sign-up or personal information to run calculations.
